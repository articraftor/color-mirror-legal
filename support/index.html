Before contacting support

  • Make sure one face is centered in the photo.
  • Use indirect daylight or neutral indoor light.
  • Avoid strong side lighting, colored room light, beauty filters, and heavy color makeup.
  • Remember that Color Mirror gives a photo-based starting point, not a professional draping consultation.

Purchases

Use Restore purchases on the plan or Settings screen when an existing subscription is not recognized. App Store payment, cancellation, and refund decisions are handled by Apple.

When contacting us about access, include the app version, iOS version, and a screenshot of the error if possible. Do not email your selfie, Apple Account password, payment-card number, or full receipt.

To manage or cancel a subscription, open your Apple Account subscription settings. For refund requests, use Apple's Report a Problem service.

Privacy requests

Email articraftor@gmail.com with the subject Color Mirror privacy request. Include the relevant selectable anonymous support ID shown in Settings so we can locate analytics or subscription records.

Settings also includes Delete local history and choices, which removes saved onboarding choices, result history, settings, reminders, and the local analytics identity from that device. It does not delete Apple's transaction records.
