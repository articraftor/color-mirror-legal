Before contacting support

  • Make sure one face is centered in the photo.
  • Use indirect daylight or neutral indoor light.
  • Avoid strong side lighting, colored room light, beauty filters, and heavy color makeup.
  • Remember that Color Mirror gives a photo-based starting point, not a professional draping consultation.

Purchases

Use Restore purchases on the membership or Settings screen when an existing plan is not recognized. Color Mirror offers auto-renewing annual and monthly subscriptions. Annual may include a three-day introductory trial only when the App Store reports eligibility; monthly has no trial. The selected plan renews unless canceled in App Store account settings.

When contacting us about access, include the app version, iOS version, and a screenshot of the error if possible. Do not email your selfie, Apple Account password, payment-card number, or full receipt.

Canceling prevents the next renewal and does not erase the free baseline or local data. Premium item checks, additional baselines, monthly practice, and Color Journal actions require active membership. App Store payment, refund, eligibility, cancellation, and billing decisions are handled by Apple. For refund requests, use Apple's Report a Problem service.

Privacy requests

Email articraftor@gmail.com with the subject Color Mirror privacy request. Include the relevant selectable anonymous support ID shown in Settings so we can locate analytics or purchase records.

Settings also includes Delete local history and choices, which removes saved onboarding choices, baseline and item-color Journal data, settings, reminders, and the local analytics identity from that device. It does not delete Apple's transaction records.
