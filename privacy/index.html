Privacy Policy

Effective July 17, 2026

Color Mirror analyzes one photo on your device. We do not upload your photo to our servers or use it to train an AI model.

Information that stays on your device

Color Mirror keeps the following information locally so the app can work:

  • Your four onboarding choices about intended use and photo conditions.
  • A selected or captured photo while the analysis screen is active.
  • A small temporary working image, face position, and derived color features.
  • Finished result history containing the season label, explanation, and date.
  • Onboarding completion, appearance preference, and review-prompt timing.

The source photo and temporary working image are not stored in result history. Temporary image files may remain in storage managed by iOS until the system clears its cache or the app is deleted. A photo selected from your library remains under your control in the Photos app.

Information sent to service providers

Product analytics

We use PostHog to understand whether people can complete onboarding, reach the paywall, and receive a result. PostHog receives a random identifier, event names, onboarding beat identifiers and numbers, and technical details such as app version, operating-system version, device class, screen size, and session information.

We do not send onboarding answer values, photos, face data, sampled colors, season results, names, email addresses, or advertising identifiers. Session replay, automatic lifecycle capture, surveys, remote feature flags, error autocapture, and GeoIP enrichment are disabled. The PostHog project is configured to discard client IP data. We do not use this information for advertising or tracking across other companies' apps or websites.

Purchases

We use RevenueCat to load the one-time unlock, validate the purchase, unlock premium access, report transaction status, and restore purchases. RevenueCat receives an anonymous customer identifier, product and entitlement information, Apple receipt or transaction information, and technical app or device information.

Color Mirror does not create an account or send RevenueCat your name, email address, photo, onboarding answers, or result. Apple processes App Store payments. Articraftor LLC does not receive your payment-card number or Apple Account password.

Sharing

When you choose Share, iOS displays its system share sheet. Result text is sent only to the destination you select. Color Mirror does not learn which person or service receives it.

Permissions

Camera access is requested only when you choose to take a photo. Choosing an existing photo opens Apple's system picker without asking Color Mirror for full-library access. Face validation and color analysis happen on the device.

Retention and deletion

Settings includes Delete local history and choices. After confirmation, it removes onboarding choices and progress, finished result history, local app settings, scheduled reminders, and the local analytics identity.

Deleting Color Mirror also removes its app database, preferences, SDK storage, and app cache from that device. Apple may retain transaction records as required to operate the App Store. PostHog and RevenueCat may retain processor records under their service and legal obligations.

To request deletion of analytics or purchase-service records associated with your anonymous installation, email articraftor@gmail.com. Selectable anonymous support IDs appear in Settings. We will use available provider controls to process the request, subject to records that must be retained for security, financial, fraud-prevention, or legal reasons. We will never ask you to email your selfie or payment credentials.

Children

Color Mirror is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly ask for a child's name, contact information, or account.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version and effective date will be posted on this page.
